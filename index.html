ABOUT

Know more about Amritangshu Chakravarty

Amritangshu Chakravarty

Certified Personal Trainer (NASM)
Fitness Nutrition Specialization
Certified Sleep Science, Brain Fitness and Stress Management Coach

Amritangshu Chakravarty is a PhD candidate in Biochemistry at the University of Kansas (Lawrence) in USA. He works on characterizing the protein-protein interactions involved in the infections of pathogenic, Gram-negative bacteria like Salmonella and Shigella using Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) Spectroscopy. Amritangshu is a Personal Trainer certified by the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) in USA with a specialization in Fitness Nutrition. He is also a Certified Sleep Science, Brain Fitness and Stress Management Coach. He has coached 150 people in the last 2 months (this blog is being written in February 2019) to help them achieve their personal fitness goals, ranging from weight loss and gaining lean muscle mass improving their quality of sleep, brain health and stress profile. Amritangshu has worked closely with doctors in case of clients with underlying medical conditions like hypothyroidism, abnormal lipid profile, diabetes, Vitamin D deficiency, PCOS and non-alcoholic fatty liver disease to give them a better life by integrating diet, exercise and wellness.

Amritangshu was born in a middle-class Bengali family in Kolkata, India and completed his Bachelors and Masters in Biochemistry before joining the University of Kansas for his doctoral studies. His passion for fitness and entrepreneurship and his urge to help people led him to complete his certifications in the fitness industry. He coaches his clients online with a personalized diet and exercise regime along with setting SMART (Specific, Measurable, Actionable, Realistic and Timely) goals. He also provides them easy, actionable steps to improve their sleep, manage stress and improve their overall brain health.
